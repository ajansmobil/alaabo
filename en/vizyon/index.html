Our Vision


Our vision is to become a global brand in the shoe industry and to strengthen our leading position in world markets by continuously developing our production technologies. With our production facilities in Istanbul, Dubai, Egypt, and other countries, we aim to offer the highest quality products to our customers and to continuously improve by following innovations in the sector. With our modern production facility of 40,000 square meters and a daily production capacity of 240,000 pairs, we will continue to be a reliable supplier in the global market.
