Our Mission


Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality shoe products and to maintain our leading position in the global market. With our expertise in polyurethane, PFC, and EVA, and our daily production capacity of 240,000 pairs, we aim to deliver products that exceed our customers' expectations. With our sustainable production processes and eco-friendly approach, we continue to be a model company in the sector.
