User Rights


Everyone has the right to request the protection of their personal data. The protection of your personal data is very important to us, and we act in accordance with the Law on the Protection of Personal Data No. 6698 (KVKK) and relevant legislation. Users have the right to be informed about the purposes for which their data is collected, how it is stored, and with whom it is shared.

You can request to update, correct, delete, and anonymize your personal data. You can also request detailed information about the purpose of use and the processing process of the collected data, and obtain information about third parties involved in these processes. You can always contact us to exercise your rights.

For detailed information about the protection of your personal data and your rights, you can review our Privacy Policy on our website. As a user, the security of your data is our priority, and we have taken the necessary technical and administrative measures.
