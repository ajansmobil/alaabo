About Us


Al-Aabo Group's shoe manufacturing and trade began in 1952 with a small shop in Aleppo that produced handmade shoes and traded them for many years. Under Mustafa Al-Aabo's management, the company began to expand in POLYURETHANE, PFC, and EVA areas to provide the best possible product quality and a well-thought-out price, and to provide consumer products for both local and foreign markets.

Al-Aabo Company grew in Gaziantep, Turkey in 2010 with the same goal: to reach the company's target of producing 32 polyurethane machines, 25 PFC, 5 TPU, and 7 EVA equivalents in a 40,000 square meter area. 240,000 pairs per day.
