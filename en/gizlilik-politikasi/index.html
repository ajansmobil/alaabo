Our Privacy Policy


When you visit our website, some information is collected through cookies in order to improve your use of our site and the services offered on our site. This information, except for your identity information, is used to provide better services and improve user experience. Cookies are used to remember your preferences, optimize the operation of our site, and provide you with a better experience.

The collected information is not shared with third parties and is used only to improve the performance of our site and our services. Users can block the use of cookies by changing their browser settings. However, you should note that some parts of our site may not function properly in this case.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. We take the necessary technical and administrative measures to ensure the security of your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, you can contact us.
